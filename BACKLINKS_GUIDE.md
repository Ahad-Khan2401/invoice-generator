**Goal:** Build quality backlinks so Google trusts the site and ranks it from
position ~65 up to page 1. Backlinks = other sites linking to yours = "votes"
that tell Google your site is trustworthy.

> **Reality:** You need ~15–30 quality backlinks over 2–3 months to move rankings.
> Do 2–3 per week. Don't buy backlinks (Google penalty). Don't spam.

# TIER 1 — EASIEST BACKLINKS (start here, ~90% success)

These are directory/profile links anyone can create. Do all 6 in week 1.

## 1. SaaSHub  ⭐ (5 min)
1. Open **saashub.com**
2. Top right → **Sign up** (use your Gmail)
3. After login, go to **saashub.com/submit** (or click "Submit a product")
4. Fill the form:
   - Product name: `PDF Bill Builder`
   - Website: `https://www.pdfbillbuilder.com`
   - Short description: (paste the 160-char one above)
   - Category: `Invoicing` / `Billing`
   - Tags: `invoice, pdf, free, receipt, billing`
5. Submit → it gets reviewed and listed. **= 1 backlink** ✅

## 2. IndieHackers  ⭐ (5 min)
1. Open **indiehackers.com** → Sign in with Google
2. Click your avatar → **Products** → **Add a product**
3. Fill:
   - Name: `PDF Bill Builder`
   - URL: `https://www.pdfbillbuilder.com`
   - Tagline + description: (paste from above)
4. Save. Your product page now links to your site. **= 1 backlink** ✅
5. Bonus: post a short intro in the "Building in public" group.

## 3. Crunchbase  (10 min)
1. Open **crunchbase.com** → Sign up (free)
2. Top right → **Add** → **Company / Organization**
3. Fill:
   - Organization name: `PDF Bill Builder`
   - Website: `https://www.pdfbillbuilder.com`
   - Description, location (Karachi), founded (2026)
4. Submit for review. **= 1 strong backlink** (Crunchbase is DA 91) ✅

# TIER 2 — PROFILE BACKLINKS (create a profile, add your website link)

Each social/profile page that links to your site is a backlink AND builds
brand presence. Do these in week 1–2.

## 7. LinkedIn Company Page (10 min)
1. linkedin.com → **For Business** (grid icon top right) → **Create a Company Page**
2. Name: `PDF Bill Builder`, Website: your URL
3. Add logo + description. **= backlink + credibility** ✅

## 8. X / Twitter (5 min)
1. Create account `@pdfbillbuilder` (if free)
2. In **bio**, add: `Free PDF invoice generator → pdfbillbuilder.com`
3. The website field = a backlink. Post a launch tweet too.

## 9. Facebook Page (5 min)
1. facebook.com → Create → **Page**
2. Name: `PDF Bill Builder`, Category: Software
3. Add website URL in the "About" / Contact info. **= backlink** ✅

## 10. Pinterest Business (10 min)
1. pinterest.com/business/create
2. Add & **claim your website** (Pinterest gives you a verification step)
3. Claimed website = a quality backlink + Pinterest traffic for "invoice template" pins.

## 11. GitHub (2 min — you already have the repo)
1. Open your repo → **About** (gear icon, top right)
2. In **Website** field, paste `https://www.pdfbillbuilder.com`
3. Also add it to your GitHub **profile** bio. **= backlink** ✅

## 12. Other quick profile links

# TIER 3 — CONTENT BACKLINKS (write once, link back — strongest)

Writing an article on a high-authority site and linking to your tool is the
**most powerful** backlink type. Do 1 per week from week 2.

## 13. Medium (20 min)
1. medium.com → Sign up → **Write**
2. Title: `How to Create a Professional Invoice for Free (No Software)`
3. Write 500–700 words (you can adapt your own blog post content)
4. Naturally link the words "free invoice generator" to your site
5. Publish. **= DA 95 backlink** ✅

## 14. Dev.to (20 min)
1. dev.to → Sign in with GitHub → **Create Post**
2. Title: `I built a browser-based PDF invoice generator with Next.js + jsPDF`
3. Write a short technical article (how the PDF is built client-side)
4. Link to your site. Tags: `nextjs, webdev, javascript, showdev`
5. Publish. **= DA 90 backlink** ✅

## 15. Hashnode (20 min)
- Same as dev.to. hashnode.com → free blog → write technical post → link back.

## 16. Quora Spaces / Reddit (no-follow but real traffic)
- See TRAFFIC_PLAYBOOK.md for ready-to-post Reddit + Quora content.
- These are "no-follow" (less SEO weight) but send real visitors.

# ⚠️ DO / DON'T

**DO:**
- Use the SAME name, URL, and description everywhere (consistency = trust)
- Space submissions out (2–3/week looks natural)
- Prioritise relevant sites (invoicing, SaaS, freelancer, business)
- Be genuinely helpful on Reddit/Quora before linking

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Buy backlinks (Fiverr "1000 backlinks" = Google penalty)
- ❌ Spam the same forum repeatedly
- ❌ Use fake accounts to link to yourself
- ❌ Put your link in irrelevant places (it gets ignored or flagged)
